Manual Book

Registration Procedure

1

Fill in full personal data (NISN, NPSN, name, email, password) and select your role: student/teacher/assessor..

2

If the biodata is validated, the system will redirect you back to the dashboard page.

3

Login using your registered NISN and password.

4

After logging in, the user will be directed to the page corresponding to their role.

Procedure for the Assessment of the Creativity Certification

1

Choose a category for your idea and upload the idea to be submitted.

2

The validation process will be carried out by the system and an assessor.

3

The validation results will be forwarded step by step to the relevant parties.

4

Passing Criteria for Certification

Assessment is based on the aspects of uniqueness, benefit, rationality, creativity, impact, and potential for realization.
